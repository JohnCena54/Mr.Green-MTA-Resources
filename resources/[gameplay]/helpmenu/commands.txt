                           -------------------------------> BINDS <-------------------------------

GC Horn
H - Press it to trigger the custom horn in the GC shop.
 
GC Bike Trails
1, 2, 3, 4, 5 - Each of these do different animations when the bike is in the air.

Sidebar menu
F1 - Show the sidebar menu.

Switch server
F2 - Press twice to be redirected to the other server.

Private Messaging
F3 - Open Private Messaging.

Achievements 
F4 - Show your achievements.

Toptimes
F5 - View current map toptimes.

GCShop
F6 - Open GreenCoins Shop window.

SoundCloud Player 
F7 - Open/Close SoundCloud Music Player.

Console
F8 - Brings up your console.

Help menu
F9 - Open/close help menu.

Player stats
F10 - Stats

Radar/Map
F11 - Open/close your map

Take a screen shot
F12
